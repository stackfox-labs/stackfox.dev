Account settings are the personal part of the Settings area. They are global to your StackFox account, not tied to just one project or one organization.

## Profile

The **Profile** card shows the identity information pulled from Roblox when you sign in:

| Field | Source |
|---|---|
| Display Name | Roblox display name |
| Roblox Username | Your `@username` on Roblox |
| Roblox User ID | Your numeric Roblox user ID |
| Member Since | When your StackFox account was created |

<Callout type="info" title="Read-only profile">
This information comes from Roblox OAuth and cannot be edited inside StackFox. If you change it on Roblox, sign in again to refresh what StackFox shows.
</Callout>

## Authentication

StackFox uses **Roblox OAuth** for sign-in. There is no separate StackFox password.

When you sign in, StackFox creates a secure session cookie for the dashboard. Signing out clears that session.

## Delete Account

The **Account Danger Zone** includes **Delete Account**.

Deleting your account will:

- permanently remove your StackFox user record and active sessions
- delete every organization you own
- delete every project inside those owned organizations
- delete the data inside those owned projects, including events, records, flows, logs, and API keys
- remove your membership from organizations you do not own

To confirm, type your Roblox username and submit the delete dialog.

<Callout type="warning" title="Irreversible">
Account deletion is permanent. If you own organizations, deleting your account deletes those organizations and their projects too.
</Callout>
