Account settings are found at the bottom of any project's **Settings** page. They're global to your StackFox account, not specific to a single project.

## Profile

The **Profile** card shows the information pulled from your Roblox account at login:

| Field | Source |
|---|---|
| Display Name | Roblox display name |
| Roblox Username | Your `@username` on Roblox |
| Roblox User ID | Your numeric Roblox user ID |
| Member Since | When your StackFox account was created |

<Callout type="info" title="Read-only profile">
Profile information is synced from Roblox OAuth each time you sign in and cannot be edited in StackFox. To change your display name or username, update them on Roblox.com and sign in again.
</Callout>

## Signing in

StackFox uses **Roblox OAuth** for authentication — there is no separate password. Click **Sign In with Roblox** on the dashboard login screen to authenticate with your Roblox account.

Your session persists via a secure, HTTP-only cookie. Clicking the sign-out button in the sidebar clears the session immediately.

## Delete Account

The **Account Danger Zone** has a single action: **Delete Account**.

Deleting your account:

- Permanently removes your StackFox user record and all active sessions
- Deletes every project you own, including all their events, records, logs, and API keys
- Removes your membership from any projects you were added to as a member (those projects are not deleted)

To confirm, type your Roblox username into the confirmation field and click **Confirm Delete Account**.

<Callout type="warning" title="Irreversible">
Account deletion is permanent. All data owned by your account is destroyed and cannot be recovered. Projects you were a member of (but did not own) are unaffected.
</Callout>
